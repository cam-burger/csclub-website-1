Centripetal Motion and Kepler's Laws Practice Questions

Centripetal Force Questions

1. A car is moving in a circular path with a radius of 50 m at a speed of 20 m/s. Calculate the centripetal acceleration. (8 m/s2)

2. A satellite orbits a planet in a circular orbit with a radius of 1000 km and completes one orbit in 90 minutes. What is the centripetal acceleration? (1.35m/s^2)

3. A cyclist is riding in a circular path of radius 15 m at a speed of 5 m/s. Determine the centripetal acceleration. (1.67m/s^2)

4. A Ferris wheel has a radius of 10 m and completes one rotation every 30 seconds. Find the centripetal acceleration of a passenger on the wheel. (0.139 m/s2)
